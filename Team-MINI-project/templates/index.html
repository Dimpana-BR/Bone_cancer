Bone Cancer Overview

Bone cancer occurs when abnormal cells grow uncontrollably within your bones. Early detection is crucial for successful treatment and preventing metastasis.

Cancer Stages:

  • Stage 1: Localized small tumor, limited damage.
  • Stage 2: Cancer growing within the bone, possibly slightly larger.
  • Stage 3: Spread to nearby bones or lymph nodes.
  • Stage 4: Metastasis to distant organs (e.g., lungs, brain).
